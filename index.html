K-MetBench Leaderboard

Public leaderboard for the K-MetBench camera-ready release on expert meteorological reasoning, geo-cultural alignment, and multimodal weather understanding.

K-MetBench evaluates more than 50 language and vision-language models on 1,774 questions from the Korean National Meteorological Engineer examination across five official subject areas: Weather Analysis and Forecast Theory, Meteorological Observation Methods, Atmospheric Dynamics, Climatology, and Atmospheric Physics.

The benchmark isolates 82 multimodal questions, 141 reasoning questions with expert-verified rationales, and 73 Korean-specific questions to diagnose modality, reasoning, and geo-cultural gaps in weather-domain evaluation.

All models are evaluated under a zero-shot protocol. This public leaderboard reports paper-aligned summary metrics cross-checked against the ACL 2026 camera-ready results.
